Overview

Purpose

This sites purpose is to showcase some of the more famous and not so famous hikes and outdoor activities to do near Rexburg ID. This might include some intro hikes and some might be some that are more advanced. It can also include campsites that people may enjoy.

Audience

The audience will be anyone who likes to do outdoor activities, from people who love the outdoors, to people who have never been. It will be mostly targeted to those who are physically able to do moderate walking to those who need to be somewhat fit to do some of the activities.

Dynamic elements

I will use JavaScript to allow a user to search for an activity using it's name, level of difficulty or activity to find what is on the site. I will also create an object of these activities in a separate JS file.

Content

Home page

Welcome to the Outdoor Adventure! For people who want to just get into the outdoors, or love being in it, you are at the right place! Here at the Outdoor Adventure, we're your one-stop shop for exploring the incredible landscapes around South Eastern, Idaho. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or just starting out, we've got something for everyone. Discover hidden gems and iconic trails: Hit the trails: We'll showcase both well-known favorites and lesser-known gems, offering a variety of hikes for all skill levels. Find the perfect path for a relaxing stroll through nature or a challenging climb that rewards you with breathtaking views. Embrace the outdoors: Go beyond hiking! Explore camping options, discover spots for whatever outdoor activity you thoroughly enjoy. Plan your perfect adventure: Get descriptions and difficulty ratings. So lace up your boots, grab your backpack, and get ready to experience the beauty of the South Eastern Idaho outdoors!

    Lunch Tree Hill
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Activity: Hiking
  • Distance: 0.5 mile
  • Location: Jackson Lake Lodge, Jackson Wyoming
  • Other Info: Lunch Tree Hill is an easy, lollipop loop trail with 110 ft (30 m) of elevation gain.
  • Source: https://www.nps.gov/thingstodo/lunchtreehill.htm
    North Menan Butte Trail
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Activity: Hiking
  • Distance: 3.1 miles
  • Location: Menan, Idaho
  • Other Info: The trail takes you from the parking area to the top and around the caldera and back. You can cut off 0.6 miles by cutting through the bowl of the butte.
  • Source: https://www.blm.gov/visit/north-menan-butte-trail
    Darby Wind Cave
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Activity: Hiking
  • Distance: 5.2 miles
  • Location: Alta, Wyoming
  • Other Info: The hike is moderate with an elevation gain of 1,800 feet. Be sure to bring a flashlight if you want to explore the cave!
  • Source: https://www.rexburgonline.com/things-to-do/darby-wind-caves-idaho/
    Black Canyon
  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Activity: Hiking
  • Distance: 12.6 miles
  • Location: Caribou-Targhee National Forest, Idaho
  • Other Info: This is a popular trail for backpacking, hiking, and horseback riding, but you can still enjoy some solitude during quieter times of day.
  • Source: https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/idaho/black-canyon-to-big-burns
    Beaver Dick Campgrounds
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Activity: Camping
  • Distance: N/A
  • Location: Beaver Dick Park, Rexburg, Idaho
  • Other Info: This is can be a good camping spot for someone who has never camped before. Just a short drive outside Rexburg, Beaver Dick park is a good size park next to the Henry's Fork of the Snake River.
  • Source: https://www.co.madison.id.us/departments/parks___recreation/beaver_dick_park/index.php
    Twin Bridges Park
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Activity: Camping
  • Distance: N/A
  • Location: Twin Bridges Park, Rexburg, Idaho
  • Other Info: The park is located on 27 acres of land and is a short drive outside of Rexburg. This can be a good spot for some relaxing camping, or for someone who is a novice to camping as it is close enough to town.
  • Source: https://www.co.madison.id.us/departments/parks___recreation/twin_bridges_park/index.php
